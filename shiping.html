FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common shipping and delivery questions.

Delivery typically takes 1–7 business days depending on your location, the product, and the seller. Metro cities usually receive orders faster. You can check the estimated delivery date on the product page of Amazon or Flipkart before placing your order.

All shipping and logistics are handled by the respective seller platforms — Amazon, Flipkart, or their designated courier partners (such as Amazon Logistics, Ekart, Delhivery, etc.). Market World does not manage any shipping operations directly.

You can track your order directly on the platform where it was purchased. Visit Amazon's "Your Orders" section at amazon.in, or Flipkart's "My Orders" section at flipkart.com. A tracking link is also typically included in your confirmation email.

International shipping availability depends entirely on the individual seller's and platform's policies. Amazon India and Flipkart primarily serve Indian customers. Please check the product listing on the partner platform for international delivery options.

Delivery timelines vary because each product may be fulfilled by a different seller, from a different warehouse location, using different courier services. Product availability, your delivery pin code, and current demand also affect delivery speed.
