The Basics

What Are Cookies, Actually?

Think of cookies as tiny memory notes that your browser saves when you visit Marketworld. They remember things like your language preference, what's in your cart, and whether you've visited us before — so you don't have to keep telling us.

Simple analogy: It's like a barista remembering your usual order — no paperwork, just a smoother experience.

Our Purpose

Why We Use Cookies

Every cookie we use has a clear purpose — to serve you better. Here's what they help us do:

Personalization

Show you products and deals that actually match your interests.

Performance

Keep the site fast, stable, and crash-free for every visit.

Insights

Understand what's working so we can keep improving.

Breakdown

Types of Cookies We Use

Not all cookies are created equal. Here's exactly what runs on Marketworld:

Functional Cookies

Required

These are essential. They keep your cart working, remember your login, and make sure the site doesn't break. Can't be turned off.

Cart persistence Session management Security verification

Analytics Cookies

Optional

Help us understand how visitors use the site — which pages are popular, where people drop off, and how to improve the experience.

Page views Bounce rate Traffic sources

Preference Cookies

Optional

Remember your choices — language, currency, region settings — so everything feels right when you come back.

Language Currency Theme preference

Marketing Cookies

Optional

Used to show you relevant ads and track campaign effectiveness. Only active if you explicitly opt in.

Ad personalization Campaign tracking Retargeting
Important Note

Third-Party Cookies

When you click on affiliate links to Amazon, Flipkart, or other partner sites, those platforms may set their own cookies. These are not controlled by Marketworld — they're governed by each platform's own cookie policy.

Amazon

Sets cookies for cart, sessions, and affiliate tracking when you visit via our links.

Flipkart

Uses cookies for user sessions, recommendations, and affiliate attribution.

We only link to trusted, major e-commerce platforms. Always review their cookie policies when redirected. Your data with them is protected under their privacy terms.

You're In Control

Your Control & Choices

You decide what stays and what goes. Here's how:

Browser Settings

Go to your browser's settings to block, delete, or manage all cookies. Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.

Clear Anytime

Clear all cookies in one click from your browser. Note: you'll be logged out and preferences reset.

Toggle Above

Use the toggle switches in the "Types" section above to visually control cookie categories.

Contact Us

Have questions? Email us anytime and we'll explain exactly what data we hold about you.
