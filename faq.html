Shopping Questions
How does Market World work?
Market World is a curated affiliate shopping platform that aggregates the best deals, discounts, and product recommendations from top e-commerce sites like Amazon and Flipkart. We research, curate, and present the best offers in one place — saving you time and money. Simply browse, click, and get redirected to complete your purchase on the official retailer site.
Is Market World an online store?
No, Market World is not a direct online store. We are an affiliate discovery platform. We do not hold inventory or process payments. When you click on a product, you are redirected to the official retailer (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.) where the actual purchase, delivery, and support all take place.
How do I buy products through Market World?
It's simple! Browse products on Market World, click the "Buy Now" or "View Deal" button, and you'll be taken directly to the product page on Amazon or Flipkart. From there, add the item to your cart and complete checkout as you normally would on those platforms.
Why am I redirected to Amazon or Flipkart?
Because Market World operates as an affiliate partner, all purchases are completed on the retailer's platform (Amazon or Flipkart). This ensures you benefit from their trusted payment systems, buyer protection, easy returns, and official warranties. Your data security and purchase safety are fully managed by these established platforms.
Affiliate Questions
What are affiliate links?
Affiliate links are specially tracked URLs that identify Market World as the traffic source when you visit a retailer like Amazon or Flipkart. When you make a purchase after clicking our link, we may receive a small commission from the retailer — at absolutely no extra cost to you.
Does using affiliate links cost me extra money?
Absolutely not. The price you pay is identical to what you'd pay going directly to Amazon or Flipkart. Affiliate commissions are paid by the retailer from their margin — not added to your bill. You get the same price (or often better deals) plus you support Market World at no extra cost.
How does Market World earn money?
Market World earns a small commission from Amazon, Flipkart, and other affiliate partners when a user clicks our affiliate link and completes a purchase. This revenue model allows us to maintain the website, curate deals, and provide free services to our users without charging any fees.
Orders & Tracking
How can I track my order?
Since orders are placed on Amazon or Flipkart, you can track them directly through your account on those platforms. Log in to your Amazon/Flipkart account, go to "My Orders," and you'll find real-time tracking information, estimated delivery dates, and shipping updates.
Who handles shipping and delivery?
All shipping and delivery is managed entirely by the retailer — Amazon or Flipkart. They work with their own logistics partners and fulfillment centers. Market World has no involvement in the physical fulfillment process. Please contact Amazon or Flipkart customer care directly for delivery-related queries.
Where can I check my order status?
You can check your order status on the retailer's website where you completed your purchase. For Amazon, visit amazon.in → My Account → Orders. For Flipkart, go to flipkart.com → My Orders. You'll receive email and SMS updates from the retailer as well.
Returns & Refunds
How do returns work?
Returns are handled entirely by the retailer (Amazon or Flipkart) based on their return policy. Most products qualify for return within 7–30 days of delivery. Simply initiate a return through your account on Amazon or Flipkart, schedule a pickup, and the refund will be processed as per their policy.
Who handles refunds?
All refunds are processed by the retailer — Amazon or Flipkart. Market World does not collect any payment, so we cannot issue refunds. Once your return is approved by the retailer, refunds are typically credited to your original payment method or as store credit within 3–7 business days.
Can I replace a defective product?
Yes! Replacement requests for defective or damaged products are handled directly by Amazon or Flipkart. Go to your order history, select the item, and choose "Replace" or "Return." Both platforms have robust buyer protection policies to ensure you get what you paid for.
Deals & Coupons
How do I use coupons on Market World?
Coupon codes featured on Market World are provided by our retail partners (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.). Simply copy the coupon code shown on our site, click through to the retailer, and paste the code at checkout. Some deals are automatically applied when you use our affiliate link — no code needed!
Are deals updated daily on Market World?
Yes! Our team curates and updates deals multiple times a day. We track price drops, flash sales, seasonal offers, and exclusive coupons from Amazon and Flipkart to ensure you always have access to the latest and most valuable discounts available.
How can I find the best offers on Market World?
Use our search bar or browse by category to find deals. You can also visit our "Today's Deals" section for the hottest offers. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media for instant deal alerts directly to your device.
Security Questions
Is shopping through Market World safe?
Absolutely. Market World only links to trusted, verified retailers like Amazon and Flipkart. Since you complete your purchase directly on these platforms, you benefit from their industry-leading security protocols, payment encryption, and buyer protection programs. We never handle your financial data.
Are products listed on Market World original?
We only feature products sold by reputable sellers on Amazon and Flipkart. We prioritize listings from brand-authorized sellers and "Fulfilled by Amazon" (FBA) products to maximize authenticity. However, as purchases happen on the retailer's platform, their policies on product authenticity and seller verification apply.
Is my personal data secure on Market World?
Yes. Market World only collects minimal data needed to improve your browsing experience (such as analytics data). We do not collect payment information, store credit card details, or sell your personal data to third parties. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.
