Popular Questions

Frequently Asked

Can't find an answer? Reach out to our support team.

How do I track my order? ▾
Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also visit the Orders section in your account to see real-time tracking updates from our delivery partner.
How can I return a product? ▾
Go to My Orders → Select Order → Request Return. Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for eligible products. Our team will schedule a pickup and process your refund once the item is received.
When will I receive my refund? ▾
Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method or Market World wallet, whichever you prefer.
Is Cash on Delivery available? ▾
Yes! Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on most products across major cities. You can check availability at checkout by entering your pincode. COD orders may require a small handling fee.
How to contact support? ▾
You can reach us via Live Chat (available 24/7), Email at support@MarketWorld.com, WhatsApp, or Instagram DMs. We aim to respond to all queries within 2 hours during business hours.
How to change my account password? ▾
Go to Account Settings → Security → Change Password. Enter your current password and then set a new one. For forgotten passwords, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.
Are products original and authentic? ▾
Yes! All products on Market World are sourced from verified sellers and authorized distributors. We have a strict quality-check process and offer a 100% Authenticity Guarantee on all purchases.
How long does delivery take? ▾
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available in select cities. You can see the estimated delivery date on the product page before placing your order.
