What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a way for us to earn a small commission when you purchase products through our links. We carefully curate and recommend products we genuinely believe in — and when you buy through our referrals, the retailer pays us a fee at absolutely no extra cost to you.

The Basics

How We Earn Commissions

When you click on a product link on Marketworld and make a purchase, we may receive a small percentage of the sale. This commission comes directly from the retailer's marketing budget — it is never added to your price. Think of it as a "thank you" from the brand for sending a customer their way.

Simple Process

Amazon & Flipkart Partnerships

Marketworld is a participant in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.in and Amazon.com. We are also part of the Flipkart Affiliate Program. These partnerships allow us to curate the best deals and products from India's most trusted e-commerce platforms and present them to you in an organized, easy-to-browse format.

No Extra Cost to You

This is the most important part: you never pay a single rupee extra. The price you see on Amazon, Flipkart, or any other platform is exactly what you pay. Our commission is built into the retailer's pricing structure, not yours. You get the same deal — sometimes even better ones we've negotiated — and we get a small referral fee. Everyone wins.

Zero Extra Cost

Our Transparency Commitment

We will never recommend a product just because it pays a higher commission. Our editorial integrity comes first. Every product listed on Marketworld goes through a quality check — we look at reviews, ratings, value for money, and real user feedback before it makes it to our pages. If we wouldn't buy it ourselves, we won't recommend it to you.

Integrity First

The Bottom Line

When you shop through Marketworld, you're getting curated, honest product recommendations — and the small commission we earn helps us keep this platform running, pay our team, and continue bringing you the best deals. Your trust means everything to us.
