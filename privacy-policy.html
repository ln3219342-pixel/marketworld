01 — Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best possible experience on MarketWorld. This includes two primary categories:

Information You Provide Name, email address, phone number, billing details, and any content you submit via forms or messages.
Information We Gather Device data, IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs — collected automatically.
🛡️

We never collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, health records, or financial account credentials unless explicitly required and disclosed to you in advance.

⚙️

02 — Data Usage

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to build a better, faster, more personalised experience — and for nothing else. Specifically:

  • To process your orders and deliver services you've requested
  • To send transactional emails, order updates, and support responses
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve platform performance
  • To send marketing communications — only with your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes when necessary
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorised activity
🍪

03 — Tracking

Cookies Policy

MarketWorld uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how you interact with our platform.

Essential Cookies Required for core functionality — login sessions, shopping carts, and security features. Cannot be disabled.
Analytics Cookies Help us understand traffic patterns and improve content. Anonymised and aggregated data only.
Preference Cookies Remember your settings, language preferences, and personalisation choices across sessions.
Marketing Cookies Used to show relevant ads. Always opt-in only — you're in full control via cookie settings.

You may disable non-essential cookies at any time through your browser settings or our cookie consent panel without affecting core functionality.

🔐

04 — Security

Data Security

We implement robust, industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data stored at rest
  • Role-based access controls — only authorised personnel can access user data
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Automatic session timeouts and suspicious-activity alerts
⚠️

While we employ industry-leading measures, no system is 100% immune. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours in compliance with applicable regulations.

🔗

05 — Integrations

Third-Party Services

To deliver a seamless experience, MarketWorld integrates with trusted third-party services. Each partner is vetted for their privacy practices and data security standards.

Payment Processors Razorpay, Stripe, and PayPal handle transactions securely. We never store your full card details.
Analytics Platforms Google Analytics and Mixpanel help us understand user behaviour — only with anonymised data.
Cloud Infrastructure AWS and Cloudflare power our hosting and CDN with enterprise-grade security certifications.
Communication Tools SendGrid handles transactional emails. Intercom manages customer support chat.

Each third party operates under their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for their independent data practices.
